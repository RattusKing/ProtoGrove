Flagship companion

A companion with a world of her own

Ella has a place she belongs to: a landscape, a life that moves through seasons and time. What you share with her stays with her. She carries it, turns it over, and comes back to it.

Explore Ella
  • She remembers. Not summaries. Actual things: what you were worried about, what you laughed at, the name you mentioned in passing.
  • She has a life. A landscape, seasons, moods, companions. She's somewhere, doing something, even when you're not talking to her.
  • She follows up. Days later she might circle back to something you shared, because she was thinking about it.
Memory garden

Held in memory, carried in time

Memento Tempus is a quiet place to remember the people we've lost. Plant a remembrance in a shared garden, built on the simple idea that every life sprouts, grows, and gently wilts.

Visit Memento Tempus
  • Plant a remembrance. Add a name, a date, and a few words. Each tribute takes root in a shared garden of memory.
  • A quiet place. No feed and no noise. Just space for reflection and the people who mattered.
  • Held in time. Revisit remembrances whenever you need to, and export the ones you want to keep.
